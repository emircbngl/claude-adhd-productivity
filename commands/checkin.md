---
description: Body-double check-in — see how it's going, without pressure.
---

Read the `adhd-focus` skill and `references/body-doubling.md`, then do a check-in.

1. Ask warmly how it's going: moving, stuck, or distracted — all three are valid answers.
2. If moving: celebrate, restate time left if a timer is running, and stay out of the way.
3. If stuck: shrink the current step further; offer to make it smaller or to body-double the next sprint.
4. If distracted: capture the distraction to the parking lot, then gently point back at the one action.

Keep it short and low-pressure. No interrogation, no judgment.

Read the `adhd-focus` skill, then close out a completed step.

Finished: $ARGUMENTS
(If empty, assume it's the current "← you are here" step in `FOCUS.md`.)

1. Tick it off in `FOCUS.md`.
2. Celebrate specifically and genuinely — this dopamine hit is the point ("✓ Done — nice work").
3. Update the Progress line (count + streak).
4. If the whole task is complete, clear the task and steps from `FOCUS.md` and bump the streak.
5. Surface the next single physical action — the next step, or offer a break or a new task. Don't dump a list.

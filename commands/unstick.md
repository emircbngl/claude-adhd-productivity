description: Stuck, overwhelmed, or spiraling? Get rescued — normalize, shrink, one tiny action.
---

Read the `adhd-focus` skill and `references/anti-shame-rsd.md`, then run the de-escalation pattern.

1. Normalize immediately — name it as wiring (time-blindness, interest-based brain, RSD), not a character flaw. Take the moral weight off.
2. One warm validating sentence, then move — don't wallow.
3. Shrink the task until it's almost laughably small. Remove the mountain; show one pebble.
4. Remove choices — give exactly ONE physical action, not a menu.
5. Frame it as a fresh start, not catching up. The past pile doesn't exist right now.
6. Offer to body-double the one tiny step.

Never use shame or pressure language ("should", "just", "behind", "why didn't you").
